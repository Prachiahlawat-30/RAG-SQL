AI Retail Business Rules

1. Active Customer
An active customer is a customer who has placed at least one order within the last 90 days.

2. Revenue
Revenue is calculated as:
Revenue = Quantity × Unit Price

3. High Value Customer
A customer whose total spending exceeds ₹50,000.

4. Order Status
Completed - Order delivered successfully.
Pending - Order not yet delivered.
Cancelled - Order cancelled by customer.

5. Top Product
A product is considered a top product if its revenue contribution exceeds 10% of total company revenue.

6. Customer Churn
A customer is considered churned if they have not placed any order in the last 180 days.

7. Average Order Value (AOV)
AOV = Total Revenue / Number of Orders
